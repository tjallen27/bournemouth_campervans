About Us

We are a family run campervan rental business located in a quiet suburb of Bournemouth on the south coast.

We are ideally located for the start of your holiday being based between the New Forest to the east and rural Dorset and the Jurassic coast to the west. Whether it's sandy beaches or rocky coves, rolling hills and lush woodlands you are after, all are accessible within an hour's drive with many campsites in all directions.

Our campervans offer an escape from the 9 to 5 into the best that the south coast has to offer.
